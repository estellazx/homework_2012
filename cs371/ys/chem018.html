Learning Electrochemistry

The importance of electrochemistry is undeniable�as humans, we literally cannot live without electrochemistry for proper cell function and transmission of signals through the nervous system. Electrochemistry is also vital in a wide range of important technological applications, such as batteries for mobile devices and vehicles. In spite of its ubiquity there are very few formal educational degree programs in electrochemistry. So how do the scientists and engineers working on electrochemical products and processes learn the electrochemistry they need?
