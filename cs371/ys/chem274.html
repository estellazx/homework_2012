Material Safety Data Sheet (MSDS/SDS) & Chemical Management

The IHS material safety data sheet (MSDS/SDS) and chemical management software brings together SDS automation, hazard communication, inventory management and more to help you reduce risk and cost while improving regulatory compliance.

  • IHS SDS and chemical management system supports multiple health & safety needs including: 

    • Right-to-Know Access: Provide an easy to access, centralized database with enterprise and site-specific information 
    • Regulatory Document Authoring: Store and manage substance and mixture information and generate compliant regulatory documents, including SDS and labels 
    • Chemical Inventory Management: Track and manage product inventory, transactions and threshold limits 
    • Regulatory List Matching: Easily analyze your chemical inventory against IHS managed global regulatory lists 
    • Automated Material Request & Approval Process: Control chemicals coming on-site with an email and web-based approval workflow 
    • Secondary Container Labeling: Create and print secondary container labels on demand 
    • Chemical Product Substitution: Compare and rank chemical products based on human health and environmental hazards and cost 
    • Regulatory Reporting: Track, compile and report on Form R and Tier II regulated substances

    This easy-to-use software brings all of your SDS and chemical data together in a centralized system to help organizations deal with the complex and ever-changing global chemical regulations.

    The IHS SDS and chemical management software and services deliver a strong return on investment for your EHS department by helping you:

    • Streamline Processes and Reporting: A centralized SDS and chemical management system eliminates manual processes, improves efficiency, streamlines data sharing and improves reporting at all levels – site, division and global enterprise. 
    • Reduce Costs & Regulatory Risk: Know what chemicals you have on site and avoid unnecessary chemical purchases and reduce compliance risk. 
    • Proactively Manage Chemicals: Avoid unwanted chemicals by implementing an automated workflow for material review and approval.

    IHS EHS & Sustainability Solutions incorporate software, data and expertise to help you efficiently manage your compliance obligations and drive your sustainability goals. To learn more about our SDS and chemical management capabilities contact us today.

    IHS SDS and chemical management software functionality enables you to comply with the following regulations:

    Globally Harmonised System (GHS)
    OSHA Hazard Communication Standard (Hazcom) | US
    SARA Title III, Sections 311 and 312, Tier II reporting | US 
    SARA Section 302 Extremely Hazardous Substances (EHS) | US

    The 2010 ASME Boiler Code
    Hosted by SDCExec, featuring ASME, TSSA, and IHS.
    Nearly a century ago, the "Boiler Code" from the American Society of Mechanical Engineers (ASME) was developed to establish standard rules for the construction of steam boilers and other pressure vessels. Born to promote safety through pressure integrity, it also furnished standards that any state could use for boilers installed within its borders. Today, the ASME Boiler and Pressure Vessel Code (BPVC) is used in over 100 countries, and is adopted (in whole or in part) by all 50 states and numerous municipalities and territories of the United States, and all of the provinces of Canada.
