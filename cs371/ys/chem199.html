Quality

We also have Application Scientists (AS) that provide support in most of the major centres across Canada. Every Application Scientist (AS) that works with UBI tests the products first in their labs on their applications.   AS' are prepared to recommend the products to their colleagues because a) the products work as well and in some cases better than their current suppliers and b) they are considerably less expensive. 

Support

In many cases you will find that your local support is in the same city, in your building, and right down the hall from where you work. This creates an extraordinary combination of low price, exceptional service, and confidence in the quality of the products. Our customers know that our products are being used successfully on the same applications by their colleagues with the same challenges that they have.

UBI, established in March of 2001, has over 300 customers in Canada.
