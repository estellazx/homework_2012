CPD COURSES

Algaculture for Biotechnology Training Course

 

If you’re currently working in algaculture, biotechnology or biofuels, or you require an understanding of the incredible potential of algae for pharmaceutical, neutraceutical or industrial uses, this hands-on two-day course provides a solid introduction for you. 

 

Delivered by leading algal scientists from CCAP (the most diverse collection of its kind in the world) this course is one of the best introductions to culturing algae.

 

Find out more about our Algaculture Course here

 

Other training courses are available, such as An Introduction to Molecular Methods for Algal Research Course and Cryopreservation of Algae and Microorganisms.  Masters and PhD courses are also available and SAMS is developing a new MSc in Algal Biotechnology for 2013.  To find out more, visit the SAMS (Scottish Association for Marine Science) website.

***** CCAP ONLINE ORDERING *****

Our online ordering system is now operational. There are two options available - either to pay online using a secure credit card payment system, or to receive a proforma invoice immediately by email, which can be paid by bank transfer, cheque (in GBP drawn on a UK bank) or by contacting us directly with credit card details.

Cultures/medium stocks will be despatched as soon as possible after the credit card order has been received or after the proforma payment has been made. *

* Please note that many of our cultures take four weeks to prepare for despatch, possibly longer in some circumstances, e.g. cryopreserved strains.
