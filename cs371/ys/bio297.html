Free Advertising for Members

In following our mission of encouraging the science, SLB wishes to help promote meetings of interest to our members.  SLB members can contact the society and request complimentary posting of their meetings and current job postings on the SLB website and advertising space in JLB (based on availability). In addition, we encourage workshop and conference organizers to approach JLB editors to consider featuring your event in a JLB Focus Section that would highlight your invited speakers to write overviews of their talks and meeting organizers would be identified as guest editors. Email us with your announcement!
