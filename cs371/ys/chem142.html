IPRI welcomes 150 visitors in Science WeekCritical community support for researchers

 
Over 150 students and community members visited IPRI during National Science Week. The community tours through the labs proved very popular, booking out in advance.


Community raised funds have been awarded in the 2012 Bill Wheeler Symposium: IPRI's Willo Grosse took out the award which will allow her to travel to Korea.
