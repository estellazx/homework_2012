About the Lab

The Combustion Analysis Laboratory at the University of California, Berkeley is under the supervision of Professor Robert W. Dibble and is part of the Department of Mechanical Engineering. We work on a wide variety of projects, some of which are highlighted on our Current Projects page. The best place to find our latest publications is the UCB Digital Repository.

We are also interested in catalysts, renewable energies, biofuels, and laser diagnostics. Please see the links above and feel free to email or contact the lab if you have any further questions.
