What is Optics? It's the field of Science and Physics related to light, colors, laser, holography, photonics, optoelectronics, spectroscopy.
Learning and working in optics is interesting and challenging: it combines visual satisfaction along with creation of engineering products applied in almost every aspects of modern technologies: DVDs, CDs, fiber optics...
