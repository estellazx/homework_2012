Main directions of research

Main activities at the Low Temperature Physics Department are directed
towards experimental and theoretical research in a field of the Condensed Matter Physics. Specifically, features of condensed matter in extreme conditions - at low temperatures, at high pressures, under the action of radiation, in high electric and magnetic fields e.t.c. - are investigated.
