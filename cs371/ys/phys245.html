Inductors Inc. is the premier franchised distributor of inductive components specializing in power and RF products. We also offer a variety of standard and custom products including DC/DC converters, capacitors, antennas, coils, chokes, resistors, toroids and transformers.


Inductors Inc. was founded in 1991. Our company is a Woman-Owned Small Business and the largest stocking, franchised distributor of inductive components in the world today. There are several distinctive factors that make Inductors Inc. truly unique:
  • A fully trained inside sales force
  • In-house engineering department
  • Inductors Inc.’s experience, knowledge, and training in inductive components is second to none in the industry
  • We offer a proprietary inductor cross-referencing technology that can cross reference 300,000 different inductor part numbers
  • Inductors Inc. provides Same-Day Shipping on stock orders entered by 3:00 p.m. Pacific Time (USA)
  • All parts are inspected and guaranteed to deliver 100% reliability and authenticity
