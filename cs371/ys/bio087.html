Testing

Clonetics™ Human Primary Cells and Media include cells that are primarily derived from normal human tissue and test negative for HIV-1, Hepatitis B and C, mycoplasma and sterility. Immuno and special staining protocols, characteristic morphology, and flow cytometry are used to characterize the cells and authenticate their identity. A Certificate of Analysis is available for each cryopreserved cell type and lot. The cells are guaranteed to perform when used with the optimized system comprised of Clonetics™ Cells, Media, Reagents, and Protocol.

Donor Criteria

Several donor characteristics are available for each lot, such as age, sex, and race. Our Clonetics™ Primary Diseased Cells have additional donor information regarding medical history and medications.

Recommended Media

Clonetics™ Media Kits have been specially designed to support the growth of each of these cell types. Each Media  BulletKit™ is comprised of basal media and a SingleQuots™ Kit of growth factors and supplements for flexibility in experimentation.
