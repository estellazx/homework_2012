Welcome to JS Technical Services, an independent service company since 1990, dedicated to providing service and parts for late model Siemens
(now Bruker) wavelength dispersive X ray spectrometers and powder diffractometers. 
We have a large stock of new and used parts and
manufacture some small parts, such as graphite sample cup bottoms and water flow monitor bushings. We also have complete used systems, with installation, training, and warranty available, as well as theory and maintenance courses tailored to your needs and taught at your site.
