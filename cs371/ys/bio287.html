International Directory of Primatology
People

The People section of the International Directory of Primatology provides contact information for individuals involved in all aspects of the field of primatology. Those whose professional interests involve or relate to primate research, conservation, education or veterinary medicine are encouraged to create a new entry in the directory.

To update or delete an existing entry, click on the appropriate action at the bottom of the entry page and follow the on-screen instructions.
