The Geospatial Modeling Environment (GME) is a suite of tools designed to facilitate rigorous analysis of geospatial data. It combines the power of the statistical software R with the geographic processing functionality of ESRI ArcGIS to drive geospatial analyses. The purpose of GME is to help users obtain better answers to geospatial questions.
