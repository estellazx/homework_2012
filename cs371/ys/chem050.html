CECRI Scientists work on gamut of problems covering all facets of electrochemical science and techonology: Corrosion Science and Engineering, Electrochemical Materials Science, Functional Materials and Nanoscale Electrochemistry, Electrochemical Power Sources, Electrochemical Pollution Control, Electrochemicals, Electrodics and Electrocatalysis, Electrometallurgy, Industrial Metal Finishing and Computer Networking and Instrumentation.

CECRI'ans has published 5500+ Papers, filed 750+ Patents, developed 250+ Technologies and carried over 600+ Projects.

Customer focus drives everything we do. Delivering value to clients is the key to our future. CECRI undertakes Sponsored and Collaborative Research projects, Feasability Studies, Analysis & Testing of Electrodeposits, Coatings & Batteries and Consultancy Services. (Customer delight)

Events related to CECRI, highlighted in the press are presented here. These include happenings in CECRI, news articles, reports, awards and honours, discoveries, research highlights, patents and publications.

The Annual Report highlights the Achievements of CECRI in all spheres of its activities. The report also covers significant scientific endeavours of all the Divisons. It also lists awards and recognition conferred upon its scientists by various academies / institutions.
