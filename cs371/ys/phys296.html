BACKGROUND                                                                                                                                                              
Sid Goodman is a licensed Professional Engineer with 50 years experience on the front line of engineering research, design, and development. WHAT CAUSED EL SID TO GO GREEN
                                                       
He left Melnor Inc. to become Director of Engineering for Hendon Co. At Hendon,he was pressured to approve a design which violated the structural code. He made corrections to the design but the manufacturing manager refused to interrupt his schedule to implement the corrections, which was to merely adjust some jigs.No cost increase of the product was involved.  Then, Sid was ordered to release a new design before it was fully checked.  His boss wanted to capture the market and  make corrections later, risking loss of life which could be caused by structural failure. El Sid checked the design anyway and made corrections.  He resigned.  
 
Melnor Inc. was glad to rehire him.  Later, he met other engineers who were also ethically challenged.   
After, he learned that what happened to them was happening on a grand scale in the nuclear industry.  
       . 
Anyone who blew the whistle on bad policy had their career harmed in some foul way. He came to understand why there were so many think-a-likes in the industry, marching in lock step.  See the page "Dead city - junk science".  It is only tip of the iceberg.

A FEW OF MANY DECEPTIONS IN THIS AGE OF DISINFORMATION
* Solar Energy requires too much area to be practical
   Fact: Hoover Dam needs a 250 sq. mile lake behind it to generate 2 gigawatts of electricity.  A solar system using the same area

* Nuclear power eliminates the burning of fossil fuel. Thus, it eliminates greenhouse gases causing global warming.
   Fact: Enormous amounts of fossil fuel are guzzled in the many stages of the nuclear power fuel cycle. Every step of the way, losses
   are sustained to the extent that the overall efficiency of this energy option is much lower than claimed. Testimony given under 
   oath was proven false.  So-called safe routine radioactive emissions ARE a serious health hazard. A massive non-routine release
   would be an enormous catastrophe. Nuclear power proliferates nuclear weapons around the world, it takes unending subsidies away from
   cleaner, safer, less costly energy sources, it destroys civil liberties and it totally undermines national security. Rather than a solution
   to climate change problems it roadblocks real solutions and presents extremely serous problems.
* The use of energy efficiency, conservation  and alternative energy represents sacrifice
   Fact: These options are real solutions to climate change problems. They will reduce inflation, ease world tensions, reduce oil imports,
   reduce poisonous emissions from fossil fuels and nuclear power, put an army of people to work, and save Americans trillions of dollars in 
   fuel and electrical costs.
