The chemical physics program at The Ohio State University is probably the country's largest, most comprehensive, and exciting.

 

 

 

Our faculty members are doing innovative work on all of the atomic and molecular properties of matter, our facilities are comprehensive, and our research environment is challenging. These qualities combine to make Ohio State's program in chemical physics one of your best choices as you seek the training and skills necessary to succeed as a scientist.

We developed this Web site to give you more information about chemical physics at The Ohio State University. In it you will find information about program requirements, opportunities for financial aid, and life in the University community. We emphasize that it can only highlight the exciting research atmosphere at Ohio State. We encourage you, if possible, to visit the campus for a firsthand look at our facilities and the people who could be a part of your career in chemical physics. We think that you will find our program at Ohio State to be exceptionally strong and stimulating.

We would be happy to send you more information and answer any questions you may have about our program or the University. The best way to learn about chemical physics at Ohio State is to speak with faculty and students during a personal visit.
