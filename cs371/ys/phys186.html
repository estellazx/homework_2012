The purpose of this site is to provide an overview of my work in fluid dynamics and fluid mixing. It includes my lectures on Computational Fluid Dynamics and Reacting Flows taught at Dartmouth College. Furthermore, you will find a gallery of colorful fluid mixing pictures, photos, videos, and an online book on mixing. This material has been created over the years, mostly through computer modeling, but also from experimental data. The main focus is mixing in industrial equipment, as used in the chemical and process industries. All images and videos are copyrighted and are made available for educational use only. None of this material may be distributed without my written permission. When citing material from this webpage, the following reference should be used: "Bakker A. (2012) The Colorful Fluid Mixing Gallery, http://www.bakker.org/cfm."
