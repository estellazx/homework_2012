We are publishers. We are scholarly publishers. We publish journals and books. They are written by scientists for scientists to support new research. They are in English, as English is the main international language of science today. All our publications are available online. Versita publishes in two models: subscription model and open access model. In subscription model, we co-publish journals with Springer and journals and books with de Gruyter. The principal destination for our open access publications is Versita Open site.
