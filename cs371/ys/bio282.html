ISCB-RECOMB RegSysGen 2012
The RECOMB Conference on Regulatory and Systems Genomics, with DREAM Challenges is now an official conference of the ISCB. The 2012 meeting is in San Francisco, California from Nov 12 - 15. It is the premier annual meeting for computational and experimental scientists in the areas of regulatory genomics and systems biology. Read more. . .

CSHALS 2013
The ISCB Conference on Semantics in Healthcare and Life Sciences (CSHALS 2013) returns for its sixth year as the premier annual event focused on the use of semantic technologies in the pharmaceutical industry, including hospitals/healthcare institutions and academic research labs. CSHALS will take place February 27 - March 1, 2013 in Cambridge/Boston, MA. Read more...

ISMB/ECCB 2013
 The world’s largest bioinformatics/computational biology conference comes to historic and majestic Berlin, Germany.   From Jul 19 - Jul 23, 2013, ISMB/ECCB 2013 will bring together scientists from computer science, molecular biology, mathematics, statistics and related fields. Its principal focus is on the development and application of advanced computational methods for biological problems.  Read more. . .

ISCB Official Journals

On January 1, 2009 Bioinformatics once again became an official journal of the ISCB. This was a "homecoming" of sorts, as Bioinformatics was the original Society journal from 1998-2004, and the ISMB Proceedings have been published in the journal continuously since 2001. Read more...


PLoS Computational Biology is an open-access, peer-reviewed journal published monthly by the Public Library of Science (PLoS) as an official journal of the ISCB. PLoS Computational Biology is run by an international Editorial Board, headed by the Editor-in-Chief, Philip E. Bourne (University of California, San Diego, USA).  Read more...
