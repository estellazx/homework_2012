X-ray Tubes and Components

X-ray fluorescence

The Oxford Instruments X-Ray Technology Group is recognized globally as a leading manufacturer of low power X-ray tubes, small focal spot X-ray tubes,
and highly stable X-ray tubes.   

For more than 20 years, Oxford Instruments' X-ray tubes have been the preferred choice for many analytical and industrial, original equipment manufactures, whom demand the highest quality X-ray tubes.

Oxford Instruments X-Ray Technology Group manufactures a wide varity of X-ray tubes including:

    • X-ray tubes which operate from 4-80 kV
    • X-ray tubes which operate up to 250 Watts
    • X-ray tubes with focal spots as small as 10 microns

 
Our X-ray tubes are ideally suited for applications such as: 
  • Thickness Gauging Measurement
  • RoHS
  • Materials Analysis
  • Densitometry
  • Elemental Analysis
  • Diffraction
  • Fluorescence and Inspection
  • Fluoroscopy
  • X-ray fluorescence
  • Spectroscopy
