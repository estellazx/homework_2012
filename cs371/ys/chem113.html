Welcome

Susquehanna’s chemistry programs offer students challenging, collaborative opportunities for scientific study. Students learn science by doing science, with the department emphasizing original inquiry.  Many students have opportunities to present their findings at national professional conferences.

Departmental faculty are scholars working on novel research projects, and thrive on student collaboration. Expectations are high, and the program rigorous, but faculty do everything in their power to help every student succeed.
